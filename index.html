About

I am a Post-training Lead & Science Manager at Amazon, where I am a founding member of Rufus — Amazon's LLM-powered shopping assistant. I trained the in-house LLM from scratch and built Rufus end-to-end, from core model development to customer-facing features, and successfully launched it worldwide.


I currently lead a team of 18 scientists owning end-to-end post-training for Rufus LLMs, covering SFT and RL to improve core capabilities including reasoning, instruction following, agent, and helpfulness. My research interests span large language model post-training, reinforcement learning from human feedback, agentic AI, and NLP.


Prior to Rufus, I was a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon Search, where I led the development of large-scale query understanding and NLP systems serving millions of customers daily. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Harbin Institute of Technology.

Post-training Lead & Science Manager

  • Rubric-based RL: Built rubric-based RL recipe for non-verifiable tasks using mixed-granularity rubrics, improving controllability and alignment consistency while mitigating reward hacking.
